get_farfield_state(direction: tuple[float, ...]) → flowstate

Returns the dimensionless farfield state depending on the scaling in use and the flow direction.

Aerodynamic Scaling
\[\begin{align*} \rho &= 1, & \| \bm{u} \| &= 1, & c &= \frac{1}{\Ma_\infty}, & T &= \frac{1}{(\gamma - 1)\Ma_\infty^2}, & p &= \frac{1}{\gamma \Ma_\infty^2}. \end{align*}\]
Acoustic Scaling
\[\begin{align*} \rho &= 1, & \| \bm{u} \| &= \Ma_\infty, & c &= 1, & T &= \frac{1}{(\gamma - 1)}, & p &= \frac{1}{\gamma}. \end{align*}\]
Aeroacoustic Scaling
\[\begin{align*} \rho &= 1, & \| \bm{u} \| &= \frac{\Ma_\infty}{1 + \Ma_\infty}, & c &= \frac{1}{1 + \Ma_\infty}, & T &= \frac{1}{(\gamma - 1) ( 1+ \Ma_\infty^2)}, & p &= \frac{1}{\gamma (1+ \Ma_\infty^2)}. \end{align*}\]
Parameters:

direction – A container containing the flow direction
